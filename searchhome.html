Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist

Microsoft Certified Technology Specialist (MCTS) is a certification given to an individual that passed the Microsoft Certification Exam.

The type of exam taken by the individual depends on the Microsoft technology he/she wants to specialize in. Example: Exam 70-680, Windows 7 Configuration .
